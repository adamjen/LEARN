### Monthly Review

At the end of each month, review:

1. What improvements have I made?
2. What patterns do I notice?
3. What areas need continued focus?
4. What new goals should I set?

## Tips for Effective Assessment

1. **Be Honest**: Rate yourself accurately, not ideally
2. **Be Consistent**: Assess at regular intervals
3. **Be Specific**: Note concrete examples in your journal
4. **Be Patient**: Progress takes time and practice
5. **Be Flexible**: Adjust your approach based on results
