## Quick Reference

### ARC Triangle Components

- **A - Appreciation (Affinity)**: Value, care, and regard for others
- **R - Reality**: Shared understanding and truth
- **C - Communication**: Exchange of information and ideas

### Tone Scale Key Points

- **+40.0**: Serenity of Beingness (highest state)
- **+3.0 to +4.0**: Enthusiasm, happiness, vitality
- **+2.0**: Antagonism, resentment
- **0.0**: Body Death (neutral point)
- **-40.0**: Total Failure (lowest state)

### Emotional Intelligence Frameworks

- **Goleman**: Self-awareness, Self-regulation, Motivation, Empathy, Social Skills
- **Six Seconds**: Know Yourself, Choose Yourself, Give Yourself
- **ARC Trauma Model**: Attachment, Regulation, Competency
